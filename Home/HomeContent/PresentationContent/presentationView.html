PRESENTATION OF THE PROJECT

...... Check4green project is structured on the idea of saveing as much energy as possible, that’s why we choose the green theme of nature. Check4Green is designed to be used in as many domains as possible:

    - factories;
    - commercial centers;
    - hotels;
    - agriculture;
    - sport stadiums;
    - personal households etc...

......Using LoRa technology, we are able to construct sensors which can communicate up to a distance of 20 km, with a minimum of electrical consumption. This technology, together with our monitoring app form “Check4Green”, a real-time sensor monitoring platform, which uses the most efficient technology possible. In conclusion, Check4Green manages to be both environment friendly and efficient in satisfying our needs, as much in a big establishment as in a regular household.

HARDWARE:

1) Gateway, creates the connection between the sensors and our cloud.
2) Sensors:

    -temperature;
    -distance;

    -amperage;
    -pressure;
    -vibration.
......We are always trying to try new ideas and innovate. Recently we’ve been working on a way to locate our devices by a technique called triangulation. Basically we are using the intersection between our gateway and sensor signals in order to find the latter’s position, which will be updated on our website’s map.

SOFTWARE:

......Monitor our devices using our web application by accessing check4green.com/logIn and creating an account for free. Once connected, you have access to our entire platform, and you can add your purchased sensors. Connect your Gateway devices to your local network through our mobile app found on Google Play, under the name “Check4Green”.

HOW DOES OUR NETWORK WORK?/ USAGE SCENARIO

......1) Deployment of a gateway or more based on the area. A gateway uses LoRa to communicate with the sensors that are within its range. This means that other networks won’t be influenced by LoRa, as it has its own frequency.
......2) Deployment of the sensors in the desired area. LoRa communication ensures that your sensors will always send date in real-time.
......3) Building a network with the Check4Green app. Firstly, the network must be created at the level of our website, this is necessary in order to give us a better control of the sensor data flow, but it also informs the user about the area of the network, the number and location of the sensors and real-time information concerning sensor events.
......4) Add sensors to your network.
Yes, you can add any type you need, temperature, air quality, vibration. You can monitor it only by accessing the network it belongs to.
