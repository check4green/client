TECHNOLOGY

Our products and services are based on LoRA technology to help provide top class solutions, compatible with the latest trends in the IoT field.

We can also customize our products to fit the requirements of companies working with 3G, LTE and LPWAN technologies such as SIGFOX, LoRA and NB-IOT.

LoRA technology advantages

- Usage of 868 MHz/ 915 MHz ISM bands which is available world wide.

- Wide coverage range: about 5 km in urban areas and 15 km in suburban areas

- Longer battery life due to less power consumption

- Single LoRa Gateway device is designed to take care of 1000s of end devices or nodes

- Easy to deploy due to its simple architecture

- It uses Adaptive Data Rate technique to vary output data rate/Rf output of end devices

- Widely used for M2M/IoT applications

- The physical layer uses robust CSS (Chirp Spread Spectrum) modulation

- Supports three different types of devices: class-A, class-B and class-C

How it works

1. Customer chooses the type of products/service they need. Our team is here for you in this stage to help analyze and determine the implementation requirements for your company.

2. Products are delivered and customer can create an account for sensor management at www.check4green.com.

3. After placing the sensors in the desired locations, the customer logs in the platform to customize the sensors and validate them.

4. From this point on, the customer can remotely monitor the sensors, set action rules based on sensors reading, customize notifications and more.
